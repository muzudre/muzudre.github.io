How to make your own weight gainer shake

Blogbot 11 Jul 2018

Three factors are important for creating a strong body, this is the amount of exercise, the amount of rest you take and your daily diet! A weight gainer is great if you need additional calories for building muscle mass, without getting to much fat.

The weight gainer shake below is easy to make at home and will give you those extra calories for building muscles. One shake contains approximately 30% protein and 50% carbs and it tastes delicious!

WEIGHT GAINER RECIPE Pbanana weight gainer shakeut all the ingredients below in a blender and mix it until you have a nice liquid shake.

80 grams of oatmeal 30 grams of whey protein 2 table spoons peanut butter 300ml low fat milk 1 banana Optional: additional nuts for extra calories You can add some cacao, honey or vanilla sugar for extra flavor.

NUTRITIONAL VALUE OF ONE SHAKE Calories: 780 kcal Carbs: 73 grams Fat: 25 grams Protein: 50 grams Salt: 140 milligrams
